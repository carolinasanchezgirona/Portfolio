About

Clinical experience, scientific curiosity and engineering.

My work sits at the intersection of neuropsychology, neuroscience and technology.

I am a psychologist and neuropsychologist with professional experience in cognitive assessment, dementia and clinical care.

Alongside my clinical work, I am studying Biomedical Engineering to deepen my understanding of biological systems, medical devices, data, artificial intelligence and the technologies shaping the future of brain health.

I am especially interested in how interdisciplinary research can contribute to earlier detection, more precise assessment and more personalised approaches to neurological and cognitive health.

This website documents my professional work, scientific interests, publications and learning journey.
